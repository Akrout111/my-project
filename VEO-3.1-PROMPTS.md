## 🎯 Design Philosophy

These videos follow a **"luxury whisper"** approach — they should feel like the kind of subtle, premium animations you see on award-winning agency sites (like Awwwards winners), not loud or distracting. The key principles:

1. **Gold + Deep Navy + Black** — Our brand palette for videos
2. **Slow & Smooth** — Everything moves at 0.5x-0.25x normal speed
3. **Subtle** — Videos enhance, never distract from content
4. **Seamless Loops** — No visible jump when the video restarts
5. **Islamic Geometric Inspiration** — Cultural connection without cliché
6. **No Text** — All text is rendered by the website, not the video
